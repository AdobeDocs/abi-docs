# Support

Get help with Adobe Brand Intelligence.

## Contact

For questions, issues, or feedback about the Brand Intelligence API, contact your Adobe account team or reach out through your organization's designated support channel.

For API-specific technical issues, include the `jobId` (if applicable) and the full error response in your support request to help us diagnose the problem quickly.
